Pasta Carbonara

Ingredients

Serves 6

  • 1 pound of fetuccine pasta
  • 6 eggs
  • 0.25 inch thick slices of pancetta
  • 0.5 cup of whole milk
  • 1 cup of parmigiano reggano
  • salt & pepper

Instructions

  • Heat pot of salted (1 rounded tablespoon) water (6 quarts) to boil for cooking pasta
  • In a large skilet, start to melt the butter. As the butter melts cut the pancetta, in thick slices and then into cubes
  • Add the sliced pancetta into the skilet
  • Mix the butter & pancetta in the skilet until the pancetta has rousted around the edges
  • Wisk the egges together with a pinch of salt & a quarter teaspoon of pepper
  • Add the milk & parmesan to the mix (the eggs).
  • Once water is boiling add the pasta to the pot, stir to avoid sticking
  • 3 minutes before the labled amount of time needed to cook the pasta (should be labled on box) is over, take the pata out of the pot & mix it with the pancetta
  • After a few stirs add the egg mix in, and continue to stir until the pasta's time to cook is over
  • Serve in plates and enjoy!
