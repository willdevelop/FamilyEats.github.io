Chocolate Molten

Ingredients

Serves 10

  • 250 grams of chocolate chips (semi sweet)
  • 250 grams of butter
  • 5 eggs
  • 5 yolks
  • 300 grams of sugar
  • 250 grams of flour

Instructions

  • Melt chocolate chips with butter in microwave
  • Wisk eggs and egg yolks while adding sugar with electric
  • Add the "chocolate & butter mix" to the bowl as you wisk
  • Shift flour onto the mixture
  • cook for 15 minutes at 160 degrees Celsius
